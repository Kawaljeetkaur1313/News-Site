Super Mario Adventure Game is Here!

Mario Game , HTML , Browser Game , Javascript , Web Development , Javascript Fun ,

28 November 2024 - Sangeeta Panwar

A new Super Mario-themed browser game has arrived! Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this fan project brings a fresh take on the classic Mario adventure. With dynamic DOM-based animations and engaging levels. In this game, players will take on the challenge of collecting coins to increase their score while avoiding a single, relentless enemy that adds a layer of tension and excitement. This game is a treat for all Mario fans. Stay tuned for updates and challenges in the news section!
